Chocolate Chip Cookie

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time: 10 minutes

Steps
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 ° F (180°C)
  2. Mix flour and sugar in a bowl
  3. Add chocalate chips and stir well
  4. Bake for 10 mintues or until golden brown
Ingredient
Ingredient Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Sugar 1 cup
Chocalate chips 1 cup
Enjoy your homemade cookies!
